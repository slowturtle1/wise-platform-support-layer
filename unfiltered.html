A news page with no editorial filter — just the raw lens of whichever AI you ask. Pose a question, pick one or more models, and compare how each one frames the same reality. The point isn't a single "truth": it's seeing the shape of each model's bias so you can judge for yourself.

This is a concept demo. The responses below are simulated illustrations of each model's general reputation and house style — not live API output and not real quotes. They're here to make the idea of "model bias" visible and debatable. For real decisions (especially how to vote), go to primary, authoritative sources. UNFILTERED never tells you what to think; it just shows you who's thinking what.
